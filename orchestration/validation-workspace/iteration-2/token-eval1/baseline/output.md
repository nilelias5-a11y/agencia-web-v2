## 3. Strategic Direction (competitive context distilled)

**Differentiation opportunity:** Barcelona specialty coffee competitors (Nomad, Satan's Coffee Corner, Bonanza) predominantly use dark/industrial aesthetics. Café Vermut's approved direction is **warm Mediterranean** — this must be perceptible even at wireframe fidelity through layout breathing room, organic shapes, and editorial photography placeholders.

**Key UX implication:** Whitespace confidence (Bonanza reference) + tactile material feel (La Marzocco reference) should inform component density and image treatment decisions.

---

## 4. UX Deliverables Required

Based on business goal (local + online sales), wireframes must cover:

1. **Homepage** — hero with local story, product highlight, CTA to online shop
2. **Menu / Product catalog** — browsable offering, filter-friendly for online orders
3. **About / Story page** — brand narrative, people, provenance
4. **Online order / Shop flow** — product detail, add to cart, checkout entry point
5. **Contact / Find us** — map, hours, Barcelona address prominent

Mobile-first. Progressive disclosure on desktop.
